Frequently Asked Questions

When is the final deadline for fall semester tuition?
The final deadline for all outstanding Fall 2026 balances is October 15th. Late fees of $150 will be applied thereafter.
How do I set up a monthly payment plan?
You can set up a monthly payment plan through the Finance portal under 'Installments'. Plans can be divided into 3 to 6 months depending on your eligibility.
My scholarship isn't appearing on my statement.
Scholarships are typically applied 2 weeks before the start of the semester. If it's still missing, please contact the Financial Aid office.
