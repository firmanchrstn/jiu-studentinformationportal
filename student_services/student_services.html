Thomas House Dormitory

The Jakarta International University (JIU) dormitory is called Thomas House[cite: 6]. This dormitory serves as a facility for fostering and empowering JIU students in accordance with the core values of Love, Integrity, Faithfulness, and Excellence (L.I.F.E.).

General & Time Rules
  • Freshmen students are required to live in the dormitory for one year after being accepted as JIU students[cite: 6].
  • Quiet Hour: Valid from 10:00 p.m. to 6:00 a.m[cite: 6]. All noisy activities must cease during this time.
  • Evening Return Time: Residents must return to the dormitory the latest at 7:00 p.m. on Mondays to Thursdays; while on Fridays to Sundays latest at 8:00 p.m[cite: 6].
Facilities & Conduct
  • A mini kitchen (pantry) is available for cooking simple meals, heating food, and boiling water[cite: 6].
  • Each dormitory resident receives 30 merit points at the beginning of the semester[cite: 6].
  • Merit points can be earned by actively participating in dormitory activities, maintaining cleanliness, and demonstrating a positive attitude[cite: 6].

Dream Blue Library (JIU Library)

Location: K-Eduplex, Jl. Ganesha2, Lot B1, Deltamas, Pasirranji Village, Central Cikarang District, Bekasi Regency, West Java 17530[cite: 5].

Operational Hours & Access
  • Regular opening hours are 08.00-17.00[cite: 5].
  • Visitors can utilize OPAC to access information about the library's collection catalog by logging in to http://lib.jiu.ac/[cite: 5].
Important Rules
  • Bringing colored and flavored food and drinks is prohibited. Only mineral water is allowed[cite: 5].
  • Visitors are not allowed to adjust the air conditioning temperature in the reading rooms without permission from the librarian. The AC temperature should only be set around 25-26°C[cite: 5].
