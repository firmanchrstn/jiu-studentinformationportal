JIU Student Internship Program

This guideline aims to establish the objectives, procedures, and management standards of the internship program conducted by Jakarta International University (JIU) to enhance students' practical skills and social adaptability through field experience related to the student's major[cite: 3].

Download Internship Guideline
Registration Requirements
  • The student has to be an active student in the current semester[cite: 3].
  • The student has to complete university courses of at least 6 semesters or 80 semester credits[cite: 3].
  • The student with a minimum GPA of 2.75. (For internal internship, minimum GPA of 3.3)[cite: 3].
  • Every student who will register for the internship must clear unpaid tuition fees, dormitory fees, and other financial liabilities of the previous semester before applying for the internship[cite: 3].
Evaluation Components
  • 40% - Work Performance & Professionalism (evaluated by Company Supervisor)[cite: 3].
  • 30% - Learning & Development via weekly reports (evaluated by Study Program)[cite: 3].
  • 20% - Self-Reflection & Internship Report[cite: 3].
  • 10% - Final Presentation[cite: 3].
Period and Credit Hour Guidelines
Classification Period Requirement Academic Credit
Full-time External Internship Minimum 18 weeks (320 hr)[cite: 3] 3-6 credits[cite: 3]
Part-time External Internship Minimum 12 weeks (240 hr)[cite: 3] 2-4 credits[cite: 3]
Internal Internship Minimum 10 weeks (200 hr)[cite: 3] 2-3 credits[cite: 3]

Student Service Scholarship (Work Study)

One of the important purposes of the Student Work Study program is to support and aid the student who is facing financial difficulties during their educational path at JIU[cite: 1].

Download Scholarship Guideline
Minimum Requirements
  • The student has to be an active student in the current semester[cite: 1].
  • The student has to complete university courses of at least 1 semester or 20 semester credits[cite: 1].
  • Every student who applies for Work Study must clear unpaid tuition fees, dormitory fees, and other financial liabilities of the previous semester before applying for the Work Study[cite: 1].
Selection Guide Criteria

The department that requests students is required to use the criteria below (Total 20 points)[cite: 1]:

  • Students' need (1-5 points)[cite: 1].
  • Suitability of required skill for Work Study job (1-5 points)[cite: 1].
  • Interview (1-5 points)[cite: 1].
  • Dormitory point (5%)[cite: 1].
