Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

Jakarta International University (JIU) collects personal data required for academic and administrative purposes. This includes, but is not limited to, your full name, student ID, institutional email, academic records, financial status, and dormitory residency information.

2. How We Use Your Data

The information collected through the Student Information Portal is used strictly to:

  • Manage course registrations, grades, and academic transcripts.
  • Process tuition fee payments and scholarship applications.
  • Facilitate campus services such as dormitory bookings and library access.
  • Communicate official university announcements and emergency alerts.

3. Data Protection

We implement robust security measures, including encryption and secure server protocols, to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is never sold to third parties.

4. Your Rights

As a student, you have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you and request corrections to any inaccurate or out-of-date information via the Academic Affairs office.
