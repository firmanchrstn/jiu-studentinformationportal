Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment

Jakarta International University is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all students, including those with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to the Student Information Portal.

Measures to Support Accessibility

We take the following measures to ensure accessibility:

  • Applying high contrast color schemes for readability.
  • Ensuring consistent HTML semantics so screen readers can properly interpret the structure of the pages.
  • Designing responsive layouts that adapt to screen magnifiers and varying screen sizes.
  • Providing alternative text (alt text) for all meaningful images.

Feedback and Assistance

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of the portal. If you encounter any accessibility barriers while using this system, please let us know so we can address the issue promptly.

Contact Student Affairs at studentaffairs@jiu.ac.id.
