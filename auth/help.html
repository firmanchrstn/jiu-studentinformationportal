Help & Support Center

Forgot Your Password?

If you have forgotten your password or are locked out of your account, you can reset it by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in page. A reset link will be sent to your registered institutional email (e.g., student@jiu.ac.id).

Account Registration Issues

Ensure that you are entering your official Student ID (e.g., SC-2024-XXXX) precisely as it appears on your admission documents. If the system still does not recognize your ID, please visit the Academic Affairs office or contact IT Support.

Contact IT Support

If you are experiencing technical difficulties, bugs, or system errors, our IT Support team is ready to help.

  • Email: it.support@jiu.ac.id
  • Office Hours: Monday - Friday, 08:00 AM - 17:00 PM
  • Location: IT Helpdesk, JIU Main Building, 1st Floor
